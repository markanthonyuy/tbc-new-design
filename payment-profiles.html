How do I get started?

Sign up for the Trial, Budget or Standard plan. You can upgrade at any time. All you need is a starting bank balance from your bank statement and you're ready to begin! We provide customizable category templates that will have many of the categories common to most non-profit organizations. Get started now!

What if I need help?

Paid accounts can receive help via email by sending us a note or question via the Contact Us page. We will respond to your question within 48 hours. If you have selected the Standard plan you can also receive help right away over the phone from our knowledgable staff. Trial accounts do not have access to support at this time, however can look for help via our Resource Center or the FAQ section.
